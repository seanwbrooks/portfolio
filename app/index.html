My Beginning | Portfolio | Bio

My journey as a web developer began in 2017.

In Intro to Computer Science in 2009, I was introduced to C++ programming.

Here’s how I got started…

Over the course of my professional life, I began to see a need for computer programs to automate the tasks and projects I was given. I taught myself Visual Basic for Applications, a programming language for Excel, to fill in this need.

It was a lot of fun building programs from scratch. I love to learn.

When I moved to Boston in the fall of 2016, I was presented with a great opportunity to start a career in web development. I enrolled into Launch Academy, one of Boston's most established coding bootcamps, where I became eager to learn more about technology.
